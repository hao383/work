About Us · 关于我们

Engineering reliable material flow

Headquartered in Zhengzhou, China, Devondi (得丰地, DFD) is a manufacturer of industrial bulk-material flow aid equipment. We help mines, mills and plants move difficult materials consistently — reducing blockages, manual cleaning and unplanned shutdowns.

Our portfolio spans air cannons, fluidization systems, vibration technology and intelligent storage & logistics management. As the authorized distributor for Martin Engineering in China, we pair world-class technology with local engineering and service.

得丰地总部位于中国郑州，是一家专业的工业散料助流设备制造商，并为 Martin Engineering 中国授权经销商，业务覆盖中国及 EAEU/独联体地区。

Core Products · 核心产品

Built to keep material moving

STORM™ Air Cannon空气炮

High-energy compressed-air blasts dislodge buildup in silos, hoppers and ducts. Reliable valves and tank designs deliver powerful, repeatable cleaning with minimal downtime.

FBC Fluidization System流化系统

Aeration pads and fluidization technology introduce controlled air into bins and vessels, turning stubborn powders into a free-flowing state for consistent discharge.

Linear Vibrators线性振动器

Pneumatic and electric vibrators apply directional force to bin walls and chutes, breaking bridges and rat-holes to promote steady, gravity-assisted flow.

Smart Storage Platform智能仓储平台

An intelligent bulk storage, transport and logistics management platform that monitors levels, flow and equipment in real time — bringing visibility and control to the whole material chain.
