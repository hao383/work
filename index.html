About Us 关于我们

Headquartered in Zhengzhou, China · 总部位于中国郑州

Devondi (Chinese brand 得丰地, DFD) is a manufacturer of industrial bulk-material flow-aid equipment. We help producers in heavy industry move difficult materials reliably — reducing blockages, buildup and unplanned downtime.

得丰地（Devondi，DFD）是一家工业散料流动助流设备制造商，总部位于中国郑州。我们为重工业客户提供可靠的散料流动解决方案， 减少堵料、积料与非计划停机。作为 Martin Engineering 的中国授权经销商，公司市场覆盖中国及 EAEU／独联体地区。

Core Products 核心产品

Engineered flow-aid systems for the toughest bulk handling

💥

STORM™ Air Cannon

STORM™ 空气炮

High-energy compressed-air blasts dislodge buildup and bridging in silos, hoppers and ducts. A robust valve design delivers reliable, on-demand cleaning with minimal maintenance.

🌀

FBC Fluidization System

FBC 流化系统

Aerates and fluidizes fine powders so material flows freely toward outlets. Ideal for cement, fly ash and other cohesive bulk solids prone to compaction.

📳

Linear Vibrator

线性振动器

Directional vibration promotes consistent discharge from bins and chutes. Durable, low-noise units keep stubborn materials moving without bridging or rat-holing.

📊

Smart Bulk Storage & Transport Platform

智能散料仓储运输管理平台

A digital platform for monitoring, scheduling and optimizing bulk storage and transport — turning real-time data into safer, more efficient operations.
