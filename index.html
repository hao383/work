About Devondi · 关于我们

Bulk-material flow specialists, built for heavy industry.

Headquartered in Zhengzhou, China, Devondi (得丰地 / DFD) is a manufacturer of industrial bulk-material flow-aid equipment. We help mines, mills and plants move powders, granules and aggregates without the costly stoppages caused by material that bridges, sticks, arches or refuses to flow.

Our portfolio spans pneumatic STORM™ air cannons, FBC fluidization, linear vibrators and a smart bulk-storage & logistics platform — engineered together so storage and conveying systems run cleaner, safer and more continuously.

Core Products · 核心产品

Four product lines that keep material moving.

STORM™ Air CannonsSTORM™ 空气炮

High-energy compressed-air blasts dislodge stubborn build-up inside silos, hoppers and ducts. Reliable, sequenced firing clears blockages without manual intervention or production downtime.

FBC Fluidization SystemsFBC 流化系统

Aeration pads inject low-pressure air through cone and hopper walls, fluidizing fine powders for even, controlled discharge. Stops bridging and rat-holing before it starts.

Linear Vibrators线性振动器

Directional vibration energy keeps material sliding along chutes, screens and bin walls. Efficient, low-maintenance units prevent accumulation and promote a steady, uniform flow.

Smart Bulk Platform智能散料仓储运输平台

An IoT management platform that monitors levels, equipment health and material movement in real time — bringing visibility and predictive control to bulk storage and transport.
